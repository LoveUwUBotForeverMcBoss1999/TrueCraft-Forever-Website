Server Rules

Follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for all players in our community.

Global Rules

These rules apply across all game modes, Discord, and community platforms.

1

Treat everyone with respect

Show courtesy to all players and staff members at all times.

2

No harassment or toxic behavior

Scamming, bullying, trolling, or any form of harassment is strictly prohibited.

3

Bug exploitation is forbidden

Taking advantage of server bugs or glitches is not permitted and should be reported.

4

No cheating or unfair advantages

Hacks, unauthorized mods, macros, auto-clickers, and similar tools are strictly forbidden.

5

Approved clients only

Vanilla Minecraft, OptiFine, Badlion Client, and Lunar Client are permitted. All other clients require approval.

6

Appropriate usernames and skins

Offensive, inappropriate, or invisible skins and usernames are not allowed.

7

Keep content family-friendly

Inappropriate builds, signs, item names, or any offensive content is prohibited.

8

One account per player

Alternative accounts and account sharing are not permitted.

9

No server disruption

Laggy redstone contraptions, AFK farming, and auto-mining are forbidden.

10

No combat logging

Leaving during combat to avoid death is not allowed.

11

Respect staff authority

Disrespecting staff members will result in severe consequences.

12

No cross-gamemode trading scams

Scamming items or currency between different game modes is prohibited.

13

Zero tolerance for hate speech

Any form of discriminatory language or hate speech results in immediate punishment.

LifeSteal Rules

Additional rules specific to the LifeSteal game mode experience.

1

Combat logging is strictly forbidden

Disconnecting during PvP combat will result in immediate punishment.

2

No alternative accounts for extra hearts

Using alt accounts to gain additional hearts is prohibited.

3

No hacked clients permitted

Only approved clients are allowed. Hacked clients result in permanent bans.

4

Approved modifications only

LabyMod, Lunar Client, and similar cosmetic clients are permitted. Advantage-giving mods are banned.

5

Player-vs-player activities allowed

Scamming, griefing, and teleport killing are permitted within the game mode.

6

Store item scamming forbidden

Scamming purchased ranks, keys, or cross-gamemode items is not allowed.

7

No harassment or bullying

We maintain zero tolerance for personal attacks and targeted harassment.

8

Appropriate content only

Offensive skins, team names, and nicknames are prohibited. Staff determine appropriateness.

9

No item restoration policy

Items lost due to server issues, bugs, or player error will not be refunded except in rare circumstances.

10

PvP is always active

Combat is enabled outside spawn areas. No claims system - protect your base accordingly.

Survival Rules

Rules specific to the peaceful Survival game mode experience.

1

Scamming is prohibited

Trading scams and deceptive practices are not allowed in Survival mode.

2

No griefing other players

Destroying, modifying, or stealing from other players' builds is forbidden.

3

Respect claiming boundaries

Building too close to other players without permission is not allowed. Cases reviewed individually.

4

Limited item restoration

Items lost to server errors, lag, bugs, or personal mistakes will not be refunded except in exceptional cases.

Last updated: September 21, 2025

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server.
